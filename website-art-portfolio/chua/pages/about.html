Hi! You can call me Zab.

I am a 20 year-old college student from Parañaque city, Philippines. I can speak English and Filipino, I love programming, math and arts!

Writing about myself isn't really my forte, so forgive me if even by text I sound really awkward! But, I also needed a kind of solid evidence of existence. So I will try my best in writing this page. Today is November 28, 2020 (sorry ma'am Ash for the late submission hehe) writing in journals are my only way of expressing my identity, so I might try to make this page a bit of a diary type.

I dedicate this blog to a better year next month. Cheers.

Ever since I was little...

I would be drawing the whole day, no matter what. I remember my childhood mainly consisting of me making pretty pictures with my beloved 64-crayon set of Crayola.

And then I went to high school, wherein the only time I have for arts and crafts is for school works. But during this time I discovered my knack for programming. Even the smartest people in school would compliment my coding skills, so I let it get to my head and now I am a Computer Science Major.

Now,

Using both of these skills together, I made this portfolio blog! Although it really needs a lot of polishing, I worked on it for only 3 days because of my busy schedule in work and the ridiculous amounts of power outages in my village.

I'm having a lot of fun coding this. I hope you're having fun looking at my stuff too!

Oh by the way, I'd like to ask how are you? Tbh I've been feeling drained lately. I got this job a while ago so that I could have extra income to buy a better laptop for online classes. It's been taking up so much of my time
Although, for the whole quarantine (which is basically the whole year na tbh), I did so much. I properly learned how to draw and sketch, and in turn I was able to translate all that I know to make digital art, I improved so much and got a small bit famous on Twitter because of it.
And then online classes started. Which was daunting at first but I was able to do pretty well at the start.
I don't know if it's the same for everyone but, the remote learning setup was 10000x more exhausting than face-to-face classes.
Nearing the end of this semester, I lapsed into another episode of depression. I wanted to talk to my therapist about it but I wasn't willing to admit that I might have gotten 'weaker' again.
But I'm okay.
So yeah, those are some of the stuff about me.
